About Daisy Treats

Baking that started with a challenge and grew into a business built on creativity.

Daisy Treats started from a genuine passion for baking. After a seven-day baking journey, that passion turned into something serious, and the business officially launched in November 2024.

Since then, the brand has continued to grow through online and in-person baking classes, customer feedback, and constant refinement in both flavour and presentation.

Every order is a chance to create something unique. Inspiration can come from many places, but each design is finished with a personal touch so the final result feels original, thoughtful, and special.

Pricing

Each order is priced around the design, size, and level of detail involved.

  • Base pricing covers ingredients, labour, and overhead costs.
  • Final pricing varies based on cake size, decoration time, complexity, and premium toppings.
  • Time-intensive or highly detailed designs may carry additional charges.
  • Prices are confirmed after discussing your exact order requirements.

Policies

Clear expectations help every order run smoothly.

  • A minimum of two weeks notice is required for customised cakes.
  • A non-refundable deposit is required once planning has begun.
  • Collection is free, while delivery is available at an added cost based on location.
  • Delivery is arranged through private transport to protect cake quality.
  • Please share any allergies when placing your order.
